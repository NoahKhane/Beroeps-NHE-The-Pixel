Pokémon Platinum Characters

Characters in Pokémon Platinum

Lucas

Lucas is the male protagonist of Pokémon Platinum. He embarks on a journey to become a Pokémon Champion, battling trainers and capturing Pokémon along the way.

Dawn

Dawn is the female protagonist of Pokémon Platinum. She is a Pokémon Coordinator who aspires to compete in contests and earn ribbons.

Cynthia

Cynthia is the Champion of the Sinnoh region. She is known for her deep knowledge of Pokémon and her role in thwarting Team Galactic's plans.

Elite Four in Pokémon Platinum

Elite Four in Pokémon Platinum

Flint

Flint is a Fire-type specialist and is known for his fiery passion. He is the first member of the Elite Four you will face and uses a team of powerful Fire-type Pokémon.

Bertha

Bertha is a Ground-type specialist. She is known for her calm demeanor and formidable defensive strategies. Her team includes various strong Ground-type Pokémon.

Aaron

Aaron is a Bug-type specialist and uses speed and agility to his advantage. He is the second member of the Elite Four, and his team consists of several Bug-type Pokémon.

Lucian - Elite Four in Pokémon Platinum

Lucian

Lucian is a Psychic-type specialist and a member of the Elite Four in Pokémon Platinum. He is known for his calm and collected demeanor, as well as his strategic battle style. His team includes powerful Psychic-type Pokémon such as Alakazam and Mr. Mime.
