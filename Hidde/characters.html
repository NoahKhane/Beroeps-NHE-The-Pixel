Characters in Pokémon Platinum

Lucas

Lucas is the male protagonist of Pokémon Platinum. He embarks on a journey to become a Pokémon Champion, battling trainers and capturing Pokémon along the way.

Dawn

Dawn is the female protagonist of Pokémon Platinum. She is a Pokémon Coordinator who aspires to compete in contests and earn ribbons.

Cynthia

Cynthia is the Champion of the Sinnoh region. She is known for her deep knowledge of Pokémon and her role in thwarting Team Galactic's plans.
