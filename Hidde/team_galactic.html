Important Characters from Team Galactic

Cyrus

The leader of Team Galactic, Cyrus seeks to create a new world free of emotions, believing that they cause all conflict. He is ambitious and ruthless in his pursuit of power.

Mars

One of the commanders of Team Galactic, Mars is enthusiastic and cunning. She often leads battles and is known for her determination to fulfill Cyrus's plans.

Jupiter

Another commander of Team Galactic, Jupiter is confident and ruthless, specializing in Dark-type Pokémon. She is a strong supporter of Cyrus's vision.

Saturn

The third commander of Team Galactic, Saturn is analytical and calm. He often devises strategies for the team and utilizes Psychic and Steel-type Pokémon in battle.
