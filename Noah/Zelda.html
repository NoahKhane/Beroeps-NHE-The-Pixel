Link

Link is the main hero in the Legend of Zelda series, known for his bravery and determination. He often embarks on quests to rescue Princess Zelda and stop the evil sorcerer Ganondorf.

Zelda

Princess Zelda is a key character in the Legend of Zelda series, known for her wisdom and strength. She is often the guardian of the Triforce, a powerful artifact.

Ganon

Ganondorf, often known as Ganon, is the main villain in the Legend of Zelda series. He is a powerful sorcerer seeking the Triforce to rule Hyrule.
