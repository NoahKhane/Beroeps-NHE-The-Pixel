Origin of Majora's Mask

Released in 2000 for the Nintendo 64, Majora's Mask is known for its dark themes and time-based gameplay mechanics, offering a unique experience in the Zelda series.

The Legend of Zelda Series

Since its inception in 1986, the Zelda series has become one of the most beloved franchises in gaming, known for its rich storytelling and immersive worlds.
