Origin of Uncharted 4

Released in 2016, Uncharted 4 was developed by Naughty Dog and serves as the conclusion to Nathan Drake's story, showcasing stunning graphics and a compelling narrative.

The Uncharted Series

Since its debut in 2007, the Uncharted series has captivated players with its blend of action, adventure, and storytelling, becoming one of PlayStation's flagship franchises.
